CHALLANGE

Swachh Bharat Challenge is an initiative to share your experiences of Swachh Bharat Abhiyan and invite other people to accept the challenge and join hands in the Abhiyan.

OUR MISSION

Swachh bharat abhiyan is a clean India campaign run by the government to achieve the goal of cleanliness and hygiene all over the India to lead it towards development. It is the ultimate way to achieve success in many fields as well as enhance the economic status of the country.

If this is evaluated by a photo-opportunity, then we would be doing a disservice to the nation. We all must come together and do this (clean India) wherever we are.

Contribute in making India clean by the 150th birth anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi
